ELSHADDI ACHEMPONG

FW • Age 20 • TBD

About

Talented forward with excellent technical skills and strong finishing ability. Elshaddi is quick, creative, and a natural finisher in the box.Elshaddai Acheampong is a Ghanaian forward who has rapidly established herself as a top talent in women’s football across multiple continents. She has starred for clubs like Gokulam Kerala FC in India, where she became the league’s top scorer, and Apollon Ladies FC in Cyprus, where she captained the side and topped the scoring charts. Most recently she joined East Bengal FC — delivering a memorable hat-trick in the 2025 Indian Women’s League to power her team to victory. Her pace, clinical finishing and leadership on the pitch make her a potent striker and a rising star in global women’s football.
