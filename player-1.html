ACACIA SMALL

CF • Age 22 • Paradise

Youth Prospect → National Team

Galaxy Sports Management is proud to represent Acacia Small, a Barbados international striker who signed with our agency to advance her professional career. Acacia is a talented Barbadian footballer and prolific striker who has made her mark in women's football both locally and internationally. A former standout forward for Kickstart Rush F.C., she also represented the Barbados Women's National Team, the "Lady Tridents," showcasing her pace, power, and decisive finishing.

Career Journey

Acacia studied at Allen University, where she balanced academic success with high-level football development. After her time abroad, she returned home to Barbados and continues her career with Paradise F.C., bringing experience, leadership, and a strong scoring instinct to the squad.

Career Highlights

  • Barbados Women's National Team (Lady Tridents)
  • Top scorer at Paradise FC 2024
  • Former standout forward at Kickstart Rush F.C.
  • Multiple trial invitations across the world
  • Allen University alumna - balanced academics with elite football
