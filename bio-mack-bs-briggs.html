MACK BS BRIGGS

Co-CEO, Technical Director & Scout

About Mack

Mack BS Briggs is a dynamic young leader serving as Co-CEO, Technical Director, and Scout at Galaxy Sports Management Ltd. At just 17 years old, Mack brings a unique combination of on-field experience, tactical awareness, and scouting expertise that sets him apart in the football agency industry. His youth football background and keen eye for talent make him an invaluable asset to both the agency and the players it represents.

Early Career & Playing Background

Mack's football journey began in Liberia, where he developed his skills as a defensive midfielder. His talent and dedication earned him a spot on Liberia's U14 national team, where he gained valuable experience competing at the youth international level. This early exposure to high-level football gave him firsthand understanding of the challenges and opportunities young players face in their development.

As a semi-professional footballer, Mack continues to play the game he loves while building his career in football management. This dual perspective—as both player and agent—allows him to relate deeply to the experiences, aspirations, and concerns of the athletes Galaxy Sports Management represents. His playing experience informs every decision he makes in his scouting and advisory roles.

Scouting Expertise & Technical Vision

Mack's role as Technical Director and Scout leverages his deep understanding of the game's tactical nuances. His responsibilities include:

  • Identifying and evaluating emerging talent across multiple positions
  • Assessing players' technical skills, tactical awareness, and potential for development
  • Building relationships with youth academies and grassroots programs
  • Conducting detailed player assessments and scouting reports
  • Matching player profiles with appropriate club opportunities
  • Providing technical feedback and development recommendations to clients

Leadership & Co-CEO Role

Despite his young age, Mack demonstrates mature leadership and strategic thinking in his role as Co-CEO. He works alongside Ajayi Adeniran to shape the agency's vision, expand its network, and ensure that every player receives personalized attention and support. His fresh perspective and digital-native approach help Galaxy Sports Management stay ahead of industry trends and leverage modern platforms for player promotion and networking.

Mack is particularly focused on empowering young players and creating pathways for talent from underrepresented regions. His own journey from youth national team player to agency leadership inspires the athletes he works with and demonstrates that age is no barrier to impact in the football world.

Philosophy & Approach

Mack's philosophy centers on authentic relationships and holistic player development. He believes that successful representation goes beyond contract negotiations—it requires genuine understanding of each player's background, goals, and personal circumstances. His approach includes:

  • Player-First Mentality: Every decision prioritizes the athlete's long-term career and wellbeing
  • Transparent Communication: Open, honest dialogue about opportunities, challenges, and realistic expectations
  • Continuous Development: Encouraging players to constantly improve their skills and tactical understanding
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Understanding and respecting the diverse backgrounds of international players
  • Network Building: Leveraging modern platforms and traditional relationships to maximize opportunities

Focus on Women's Football

Mack is a passionate advocate for women's football and plays a key role in Galaxy Sports Management's commitment to gender equality in player representation. He recognizes that female footballers face unique challenges in accessing professional opportunities and works tirelessly to create pathways for talented women to reach the highest levels of the sport.

His work has helped secure placements for female players in leagues across Europe, Africa, and beyond. He continues to expand the agency's network of contacts in women's football, ensuring that Galaxy Sports Management remains at the forefront of providing opportunities for female athletes.

Vision for the Future

Looking ahead, Mack is committed to expanding Galaxy Sports Management's global reach while maintaining the personalized service that defines the agency. His goals include developing stronger partnerships with top-tier academies, increasing the agency's presence in emerging football markets, and continuing to break down barriers for underrepresented players.

Mack's combination of playing experience, scouting acumen, and entrepreneurial vision positions him as a rising leader in football representation. His work continues to open doors for talented players worldwide while setting new standards for what it means to be a player-focused agency in the modern football landscape.
