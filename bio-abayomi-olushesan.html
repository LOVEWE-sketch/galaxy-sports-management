ABAYOMI OLUSHESAN

Legal Counsel

About

Abayomi Olushesan serves as Legal Counsel for Galaxy Sports Management Ltd, providing expert legal guidance across all aspects of football representation, contract negotiations, and regulatory compliance. With deep expertise in sports law and player representation, Abayomi ensures the agency operates with the highest standards of legal and ethical integrity.

Legal Expertise

As Legal Counsel, Abayomi oversees contract drafting and negotiation, regulatory compliance, player rights protection, and dispute resolution. His comprehensive understanding of international football regulations, FIFA statutes, and transfer procedures enables Galaxy Sports Management to navigate complex legal landscapes with confidence. He ensures every player representation agreement, transfer contract, and partnership arrangement adheres to industry best practices and legal standards.

Professional Philosophy

Abayomi believes that robust legal frameworks are essential for protecting player interests and building sustainable football careers. His approach combines meticulous attention to detail with strategic thinking, ensuring clients receive comprehensive legal protection while maintaining flexibility for career growth and opportunity maximization.

Role at Galaxy Sports Management

In his capacity as Legal Counsel, Abayomi is responsible for:

  • Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating player representation agreements and transfer contracts
  • Ensuring compliance with FIFA regulations, national football association rules, and international transfer protocols
  • Protecting player rights and interests throughout contract negotiations and career transitions
  • Managing legal risk and providing strategic counsel on business development initiatives
  • Resolving disputes through mediation, arbitration, and legal proceedings when necessary
  • Advising on intellectual property, image rights, and commercial endorsement agreements

Commitment to Excellence

Abayomi is dedicated to maintaining Galaxy Sports Management's reputation for ethical representation and legal excellence. His proactive approach to legal counsel ensures the agency anticipates and addresses potential challenges before they become obstacles, creating a secure foundation for player career development and organizational growth.
