About Santiago

Santiago De Sousa is a talented Venezuelan striker who recently made the significant step of signing his first professional contract with KAPA Kajanni in Finland's 5th division (Kolmonen). This represents a major milestone in his career and showcases Galaxy Sports Management's commitment to placing players in competitive European leagues.

Playing Style

Santiago is a clinical finisher with exceptional positioning inside the box. His playing style is characterized by:

  • Clinical Finishing: Natural goalscorer with both feet and in the air
  • Work Rate: Tireless pressing and off-the-ball movement
  • Physical Presence: Strong in hold-up play and aerial duels
  • Link-up Play: Capable of bringing teammates into play
  • Adaptability: Can play as lone striker or in a two-striker system

Career Journey

Born in Venezuela, Santiago developed his skills in South American football before seeking opportunities in Europe. His journey with Galaxy Sports Management began when he was identified as a promising talent looking for professional opportunities abroad.
