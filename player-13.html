Clinical Striker with Impressive Record

Alieu Sheriff is a prolific 27-year-old Liberian-Dutch forward currently playing for Ethnikos Assia in Cyprus. Born in Monrovia, Liberia to Liberian parents on September 2, 1997, Alieu later moved to the Netherlands where he developed his football career while pursuing higher education.

Recent Form & Achievements

Alieu continues his impressive goal-scoring form from last season, where he netted 11 goals in just 16 matches despite spending considerable time on the sidelines due to injuries. In his club's first official pre-season friendly, it took him just 45 minutes to register a brace in Ethnikos Assia's commanding 4-0 victory over fellow Cyprus side Doxa PALAIO Oxoy.

Physical & Playing Style

Standing at an imposing 6'2" (188cm), Alieu combines physical presence with clinical finishing ability. His height advantage makes him a dominant force in aerial duels, while his intelligent movement and positioning ensure he's always a threat in the penalty area. Despite injury setbacks, his goal-scoring record demonstrates his natural finishing instinct and ability to make an immediate impact.

Education & Character

Beyond football, Alieu is a graduate of Manchester Metropolitan University where he earned degrees in Law and Business. This educational background demonstrates his intelligence, discipline, and ability to balance professional football with academic excellence. His university education provides him with valuable skills for life both on and off the pitch.

International Ambitions

Alieu has consistently announced his intention to represent Liberia at international level, aspiring to wear the shirt of the Lone Star. His dual nationality (Liberian-Dutch) and strong connection to his Liberian roots make him a potential asset for the national team, bringing European football experience and a proven goal-scoring record.

Career Highlights

  • Ethnikos Assia (2021/22): 11 goals in 16 games, Top Scorer
  • Coca-Cola Cup Cyprus: Runners-up medal (2021/22)
  • Glossop North End (2019/20): 9 goals in 19 games, Top Scorer
  • Multiple top scorer awards across England, Wales, and Cyprus
  • Graduate of Manchester Metropolitan University (Law & Business)
  • Qualified for Europa League with Bangor City (2017/18)
  • Huws Gray Alliance Cup winner with Flint Town United (2017/18)

Career Journey

Alieu's football journey has taken him across multiple countries and leagues. From youth football in England with FCUOM U21 and Chadderton U21 (where he scored 23 goals in 30 games), he progressed through senior football in Wales with clubs like Bangor City, Flint Town United, and Denbigh Town. His move to English non-league football with Glossop North End saw him become their top scorer before making the successful transition to Cyprus football with Ethnikos Assia, where he has established himself as one of the league's most dangerous strikers.
